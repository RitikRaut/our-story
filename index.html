Chapter I

The Day Everything Changed

The world hadn't changed, but the light inside it had shifted, and I realised, quietly and completely, that nothing would ever quite be the same again. Nor did I want it to be.

Chapter II

Learning the Language of You

Love, I discovered, is its own quiet vocabulary. Every small detail of you became a treasure — a tiny private universe I felt honoured to explore, one careful, joyful day at a time.

Chapter III

The Adventures We Wrote Together

Chapter IV

Here, Now, Always

I am not afraid of the future because I know you are in it. And wherever that takes us, I know this: loving you is the greatest thing I have ever done.
